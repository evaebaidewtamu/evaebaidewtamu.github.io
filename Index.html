Seattle Sounders Football Club

Seattle Sounders FC are an American professional soccer club based in Seattle, Washington.

Sounders FC Lead, Serve & Invest in the community. We lead by driving awareness and engagement around key community initiatives. We serve as individuals around community causes that are important where we live, work & play. We invest as an organization through RAVE Foundation.
